# Acme Financial Services Security RFP

Acme Financial Services is evaluating a secure document workflow for regulated proposal, underwriting and client-service teams. The buyer needs an answer pack within 48 hours so its security, legal and procurement reviewers can decide whether the vendor is safe to invite into a paid pilot.

## Response Requirements

1. Explain how customer documents are encrypted in transit and at rest, including key management boundaries and operational ownership.
2. Provide the latest SOC 2 evidence status and state whether a bridge letter is available for the current review period.
3. Describe data retention controls, including customer-configurable deletion windows and default retention behavior.
4. Confirm whether EU customer documents can be processed in a region-restricted workflow without unsupported residency claims.
5. Describe the product capability for generating PDF risk summaries, including review requirements before customer delivery.
6. Summarize incident response notification targets and the review cadence for the incident response policy.
7. Identify how subprocessors are reviewed, disclosed and updated for customers.
8. Provide a realistic implementation timeline for a controlled pilot, including dependencies that could block launch.
9. State whether uptime or support commitments require commercial approval before being included in an RFP response.

## Evaluation Notes

Acme will score responses higher when every answer cites a current evidence source, distinguishes product capability from future roadmap, and marks any high-risk commitment for human approval before the final pack is sent.
